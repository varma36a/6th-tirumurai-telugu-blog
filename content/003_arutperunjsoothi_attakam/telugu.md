ఎఴుసీర్క్ కఴినెడిలADI ఆచార్య విరుత్తం

తిరుచ్చిఱ్టam

1. అరులు పéreugina velugu, arulu perugina lokamuna,

   arulu perugina unnata sthalamu meeda, nilichi sthiramaga;

   arulu perugina peetamu, arulu perugina roopamuna,

   arulu perugina tirunilayamuna amararu;

   arulu perugina gamyamu, arulu perugina nidhi,

   arulu perugina siddhi — na amritamu;

   arulu perugina aanandamu, arulu perugina sukhamu;

   arulu perugina jyothi — na raaju.

2. వంశమu రెండు భాగamulu, okati aparimitamaina bhagamuna,

   akkada koti-kotlu unnayi;

   kanipinche dehamu yokka bhagamulu aniti,

   prati bhagamuna aneka padardhamulu koodutayi;

   lopala, baahya, pai unna sthalamulanu veyakunda,

   nijamaina jnaana aanandam velugutundi;

   sima leni, antam leni aa tatvamulo adi velugutundi;

   arulu perugina jyothi — na raaju.

3. kannu moolamuna, manasamu moolamuna, indriyala

   dwara bhagamu, karuvu moolamuna;

   ennum moolamuna purusha taramu, paramu moolamuna,

   okati parampara grahimpadanochetu;

   aakasamu moolamuna parai, parabara telivitho

   veluvadaniki kashtamani telisukunna vaaru;

   anuvu moolamuna samstapinchi, kattukoni egurunu;

   arulu perugina jyothi — na raaju.

4. aa nilai korina, telusukoni dharincha,

   kannu adiki yetu raadu;

   disa disa vedamulu tirigi tirigi vachina adi svabhavam

   elaa palukutam — koncham kuda palakam;

   naaku palukutamani na jihva kuda;

   adi teliyani — adi telisina vaaru stutinche;

   arulu perugina jyothi — na raaju.

5. suddha vedamulu maunamena? leda suddha

   siddhanta rasamena?

   nitya nadanta nilai anubhavamena?

   aneka antamula meeda antam velasina?

   buddhi-kari samarasattatva svabhavam teliyadu;

   alanti telisina vaaru paliki paliki stutinche;

   arulu perugina jyothi — na raaju.

6. okkade naa? leda anekamaa? sadaa?

   svabhavikamaa? krutrimamaa? chitaa?

   deham naa? saamaanyamaa? viseshamaa? stri naa?

   velugunu purusha naa? adi naa?

   yogamena? viyogamena? jyotiyena? aakasamena?

   palukuvadi ento telusukoni;

   aagamamto paliki nilichi stutinche;

   arulu perugina jyothi — na raaju.

7. tattvamulu aniti pratyekam ga daati,

   tattvaateetam meeda nilaiyil;

   siddhiyal aniti telusukoni avi meeda

   siva nilai telusukoni vellamu;

   aa anma nilaiyil memu ma unarvu kuda

   koodi karaini poyamu — alanti telisina vaaru stutinche;

   arulu perugina jyothi — na raaju.

8. ekkada velugunu chitsabai sthalamuna,

   idi-adi ani palukadam kashtam ayyi;

   oka svabhavika anubhavam icchi nannu tanmayam chesi;

   aananda bhoga bhogiyanai na hrudayamuna buddhi icchi;

   kaiyilo pandi poyina podi amarndu arul chesina;

   arulu perugina jyothi — na raaju.
